Yes, the board has an obligation to inform the employees of their rights to have employee-elected representatives on the board. This requirement is specified in the representation regulations, which state that the board must provide information about its employees' right to elect representatives to the board and how the election process works. Furthermore, the board must ensure that the election process is transparent and meets the requirements of the representation regulations. So, in short, the board must inform the employees of their right to employees' representation and ensure that the election process is carried out according to the regulations.
